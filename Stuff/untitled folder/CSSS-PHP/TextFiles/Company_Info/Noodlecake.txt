<b>Noodlecake Studios</b>

Noodlecake Studios is a small indie game studio based in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada. Founded in early 2011, Noodlecake found success with its first game, Super Stickman Golf, and has since expanded into publishing, releasing games on multiple platforms. Noodlecake has published over 20 titles across multiple app stores and are currently hard at work on Super Stickman Golf 2!
